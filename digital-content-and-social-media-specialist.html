Digital Content & Social Media Specialist

Bellarosa House, Moyne Drive, Nyali, Mombasa Full Time

Position Summary

We are seeking a versatile and creative Digital Content & Social Media Specialist to join our dynamic team. This role requires a skilled individual who can create high-quality content, manage social media platforms, as well as manage designing roles. The ideal candidate will have a blend of expertise in content creation, social media management, and graphic design to elevate Sote Hub’s digital presence and engage our community effectively.


Key Responsibilities

Content Creation & Strategy
  • Develop and implement a social media strategy that aligns with Sote Hub’s mission, goals, and brand voice.
  • Create engaging content for various platforms, including social media posts, blog articles, videos, infographics, and other multimedia.
  • Support production of creative visual assets, including promotional graphics, banners, and event materials.
  • Maintain a consistent and on-brand tone across all digital communications.
Social Media Management
  • Manage and oversee day-to-day operations of Sote Hub’s social media accounts, including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and TikTok.
  • Grow and engage our online community by responding to comments, messages, and inquiries in a timely manner.
  • Develop content calendars and schedule posts to maintain consistent online engagement.
  • Stay updated on social media trends and best practices to ensure our content is fresh and impactful.
Graphic Design & Branding
  • Create high-quality graphics, including promotional banners, social media posts, and marketing collateral.
  • Ensure visual consistency across all online platforms by adhering to Sote Hub’s branding guidelines.
  • Design assets for internal and external communications, events, and campaigns.
Campaign Management
  • Collaborate with external and internal teams to create and execute digital marketing campaigns.
  • Monitor key performance indicators for social media platforms, content, and campaigns.
  • Use data insights to optimize content and campaigns, ensuring higher engagement and reach.
  • Liaise with startups, SMEs, donors, supporters and other stakeholders to bring out their voice in our media campaigns including arranging for interviews.
Collaboration & Reporting
  • Work closely with the programs and innovations teams to align social media and content strategies with broader organizations projects and programs goals.
  • Provide regular reports on social media performance, including engagement rates, follower growth, and content impact.

Qualifications and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media, or a related field.
  • At least 2 years of experience managing social media platforms or communications for a brand or organization.
  • Proven ability to create compelling content for media and digital platforms.
  • Excellent storytelling and content creation skills, including photography and video editing.
  • Proficiency in design tools (Adobe Creative Suite, Canva.) and social media management tools (Hootsuite, Meta Business Suite, etc.)
  • Strong understanding of platform-specific algorithms and trends.
Core Competencies
  • Creativity and strong attention to detail.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently, manage multiple projects, and meet deadlines.
  • Strong collaboration skills, with the ability to work across teams.
  • Passion for innovation, entrepreneurship, and social impact.

What We Offer

  • The opportunity to contribute to impactful projects and drive meaningful change.
  • A creative and supportive work environment.
  • Opportunities for personal and professional growth within a fast-growing organization.
  • Competitive compensation package.

Company Detail

Sote Hub is a leading Entrepreneur Support Organization (ESO) and a strong voice in the Blue Economy sector in Kenya and East Africa. Based at the Kenyan Coast in Mombasa, our mission is to grow a viable pipeline of ocean and inland water startups into industry leaders. Over the last three years, we have worked with and supported over 800 MSMEs, including 300 high-value startups that are thriving in the Blue Economy, climate and digital spaces. Our approach includes business incubation and acceleration, guiding startups from ideation to market. Our priority sectors now and in the next five years are: Blue Economy, Climate Smart/Nature-based solutions, Digitization and Agriculture
